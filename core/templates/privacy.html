Privacy Policy

We process personal data where necessary to take steps requested by you before entering a contract, to perform a contract, to comply with legal obligations, or to pursue legitimate interests that do not override your rights and freedoms.

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers where required for hosting, payments, administration, or compliance, and only to the extent necessary for those services.

We keep personal data only for as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, or for as long as required by contract, law, audit, or dispute-resolution duties.

Depending on your location and the applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to, or receive a copy of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent where processing depends on consent.

We may use limited cookies or similar technologies for core site functionality, analytics, and security. Where required, we will request consent before using non-essential technologies.

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

We may update this policy from time to time. The version published on this page is the current version.
